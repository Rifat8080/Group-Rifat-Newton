Here's a time line of Nelson Mandela's life:

  • 1918 - Born in Mvezo, South Africa
  • 1988 - Won the Sakharov Prize
  • 1990 - Won the Bharat Ratna
  • 1990 - Won the Lenin Peace Prize
  • 1992 - Won the Nishan-e-Pakistan
  • 1993 - Won the Nobel Peace Prize
  • 2002 - Won the Presidential Medal of Freedom
  • 2013 - Died at the age of 95
